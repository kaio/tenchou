TenChou (店長) - Data Mining Tools for Retail Shop Managers

TenChou is an Android application which performs data mining for retail shop managers. Based on data gathered from daily shop operations, shop managers could derive information and adjust their business strategies.

Dependencies

Android 2.0 or above
